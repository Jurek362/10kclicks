Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines the principles regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data by Jurek36 concerning the game 10k Clicks.

We assure you that your privacy is our highest priority. Therefore, we are pleased to inform you that our game has been designed in such a way as to not collect any of your personal data.

---

1. Types of Data Collected

In the context of the operation of the game 10k Clicks, Jurek36 does not collect any personal data or information enabling user identification.

This means that we do not collect, process, or store the following information:

  • Your identification data (e.g., name, surname, email address, IP address).
  • Demographic data (e.g., age, gender, location).
  • Game usage data (e.g., in-game progress, statistics, preferences, time spent in game).
  • Device data (e.g., device type, operating system, unique device identifiers).
  • Online activity information, including tracking and analytical data.

We do not use any analytical tools, tracking systems, cookies, or similar technologies to monitor user interaction with the game.

---

2. Purpose of Data Collection

As we do not collect any personal data, there are no purposes for its processing or use. The game functions completely autonomously on the user's device, without the need for any data exchange with external servers.

---

3. Sharing Data with Third Parties

Due to the lack of personal data collection, Jurek36 does not share any data with third parties, including business partners, analytical service providers, or advertisers.

---

4. Access to Device Functions

The game 10k Clicks may require basic access to your device's functions, such as the ability to save game state locally. This access is strictly limited to functionalities necessary for the proper operation of the game and is not used to collect or transmit any data outside your device.

---

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be subject to future changes. Any modifications will be published on this page. In the event that we decide to collect any personal data in the future, users will be clearly informed, and the required consents will be obtained before such changes are implemented.

We encourage you to regularly review this Privacy Policy to familiarize yourself with its current version.
